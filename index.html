ABOUT Me

Frontend Developer

Hello! I’m Ajay Shekade, a passionate Data Science enthusiast and a Computer Science student at Mumbai University. My journey into the world of data science began with a strong interest in understanding how data can be transformed into powerful insights. I firmly believe that data is a key driver in solving complex real-world problems, and I am dedicated to mastering the skills needed to make meaningful contributions in this field. Currently, I am developing my expertise in areas such as data analysis, machine learning, and data visualization. I am proficient in languages and tools like Python, R, SQL, Tableau, and TensorFlow. I have worked on several academic and personal projects, including predictive modeling, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and building machine learning models that can predict trends and outcomes based on real-world datasets. My academic journey has also led me to dive deep into topics like statistical analysis, data wrangling, and the development of algorithms that optimize data-driven decisions. As a result, I have developed a keen understanding of how to analyze, interpret, and visualize data for better decision-making. With a clear vision of becoming a skilled Data Scientist, I am committed to continuous learning and improving my skillset. I am excited about applying my knowledge to contribute to innovative solutions in the fields of AI, machine learning, and data science.
